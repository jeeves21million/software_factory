# Product Request

We are building **PulseBudget**, a web app for couples who want shared visibility into spending, bills, and savings goals.

## Goals
- Help couples avoid end-of-month surprises.
- Make weekly money check-ins simple (under 10 minutes).
- Increase monthly savings consistency.

## Platform
- Web app first (desktop + mobile responsive).

## Must-Have Screens
- Onboarding
- Dashboard
- Transactions
- Goals
- Weekly Summary
- Settings
